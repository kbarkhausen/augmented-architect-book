# Product Context

## Overview
TechFlow CRM helps B2B SaaS companies manage customer relationships.

## Core Entities
- **Account**: A customer company
- **Contact**: A person at an account
- **Opportunity**: A potential deal
- **Activity**: Interactions with contacts

## User Types
- **Admin**: Full system access
- **Sales Rep**: Own opportunities, shared accounts
- **Manager**: Team oversight, reporting

## Key Workflows
1. Lead → Opportunity → Won/Lost
2. Activity logging and history
3. Pipeline management and forecasting
